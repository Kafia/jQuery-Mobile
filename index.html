Our Process

Ryze Analytics process is collaborative and not rigid. Our personalized approach is centered on adapting to each client’s distinct, evolving needs

Click on each heading below to reveal details

Discover | Becoming partners with our clients

We believe every client deserves a customized approach in solving challenges around data analytics because every business works in a different context, with varying requirements. This is why when we start working with clients, we take time to deeply understand their needs, objectives, and outcomes. More importantly we listen to clients until we fully discover and grasp their strategic intent. Developing a close collaborative partnership with clients allows us to align our solutions to your ambitions. The result is a methodology that makes your success our focus.

Cultivate | Developing a pathway to overcome challenges

We cultivate everything we’ve learnt about your needs by analyzing the strategic intent and uncovering the challenges it reveals. At this stage, we question everything - our client’s thinking and our own assumptions. We exhaustively experiment with the data, analytical methods, and visual designs. Experiments lead to precise insights. From here, insights serve as the backbone of developing a strategic strategy that becomes the pathway towards meeting your objectives. We deliver a blueprint and demonstrate how the data strategy we propose will create organizational value, impact, and performance.

Construct | Building with precision

When all stakeholders have aligned themselves to the blueprint, we incorporate feedback from the process and begin constructing prototypes. Our interdisciplinary teams are scalable for projects of all shapes and sizes allowing for efficient collaboration with the client and each other. We aggressively conduct tests against hypothesis to ensure we never lose sight of the strategic intent uncovered during the discovery process. The result is fulfilling client’s needs successfully while delivering the blueprint with precision.

Deploy | Driving change with full support

Once we deliver one the blueprint, we don’t leave clients at the deep end. Implementing on data strategy insights and improving performance consistently takes intentionality without which bringing about change would be impossible. This is why we come alongside our clients and provide a deployment plan that leads to sustainable performance improvements. Our support includes training, change management strategy, establishing new processes and much more. Everything we do is tailored to each clients unique deployment needs.
